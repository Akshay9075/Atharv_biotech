Our History

Over Two Decades of Excellence

Atharv Seed Company was established in 1998 with a simple mission: to provide farmers with the highest quality onion seeds for healthy, productive crops. What began as a small family operation has blossomed into one of the region's most trusted seed suppliers.

Over the years, we've cultivated strong relationships with farmers across the country, learning from their experiences and continuously innovating our seed varieties to meet the challenges of changing climates and market demands.

Rooted in Trust, Growing with Innovation
1998

Company founded with a focus on quality onion seeds

2005

Introduced our first hybrid onion seed variety

2012

Expanded operations to serve farmers across multiple states

2020

Launched disease-resistant seed varieties developed in our research farm

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of high-quality seeds that empower farmers to achieve sustainable agricultural success and food security for our nation.

Our Mission

To develop, produce, and distribute superior quality onion seeds through continuous research and innovation, while maintaining fair practices that benefit both farmers and the environment.

Quality Standards & Certifications

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our quality management system is certified to international standards, ensuring consistent product quality.

Seed Certification

All our seeds meet the certification standards set by the National Seeds Corporation of India.

Quality Testing

Every seed batch undergoes rigorous testing for germination rate, purity, and disease resistance.
